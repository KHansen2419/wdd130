Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this webiste is education people about an upcoming craft fair. It will educate crafters who want to sell their products at the fair, as well as the people looking to buy things from the fair.

Audience

Our audience is crafters to sell their products. And our clientele is people who are interested in crafts or homemade goods. They cannot be afraid of the higher price tag since people are making the items.

Content

Home page

Welcome to the Snake River Craft Fair. A semiannual event where crafters and vendors come together for their love of homemade items. Come and experience the creativity and inspiration for yourself!

Images for the Home page

Location/Hours

This event is held semiannually, in March and October, during the third saturday of the month. The event will start at 9am and will close at 6pm. It is a free admission event, and you can come and go as you please. The local event is held at Madison Senior High School.

Images for the Page 2

Become a Vendor

Are you looking to sell your handmade goods? The Snake Valley Craft Fair is a great place to expand your homemade store locally. You can talk with locals and sell your products at the same time.
