Become a Vendor

Are you looking to sell your handmade goods? The Snake Valley Craft Fair is a great place to expand your homemade store locally. You can talk with locals and sell your products at the same time.

Becoming a Vendor

You can become a vendor through some easy steps! This craft fair is known in error and has a lot of success for our vendors. If your items are handmade/homemade or homegrown, they can be sold at this craft fair. We accept all different types of crafts, books, food, etc.

What we require

We will require you to give a full list of where you receive your items and materials for your handmade projects. You will need to tell us the type of homemade good you are wanting to sell, pictures will help. You make your setup, meaning we will provide the space, but you must provide everything else to make your display. Our booth rent is based on how much you sell. If you sell over $150 in product then your booth rent will be 4% of your sales. If you make less than $150, we will require a flat rate of $10. If you are selling food, we will need the nutritional value and index of all your food items and a list of ingredients.
