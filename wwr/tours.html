Camping Tours

At Dry Oar Bar, we offer different packages for your adventure needs. We offer a variety of Camping Tours with a certified travel guide.

2 days/1 night-

Perfect for beginning campers. Whether you are short on time or not a seasoned camper, this is the perfect package. Getting your "feet wet" in your first adventure is a great opportunity. Camping gear is available for rent. Select Book Now for more details, or contact us with questions!

4 days/3 nights-

This package is great for those who want to spend a few days in the mountains. A certified travel guide will take you hiking through some of the prettiest forests in the Colorado mountains. You will be backpacking, carrying your gear, and camping in two designated areas. Lose yourself in the nighttime views and hike breathtaking trails throughout the day, or join with a white water rafting package. Camping gear is available for rent. Select Book now for more details, or contact us with questions!

8 days/7 nights

If you want to get the full experience and camp for a whole week, this package is for you. You will camp at two designated posts with a licensed tour guide. Your days can be spent hiking, rafting, or enjoying the scenic views. Camping gear is available for rent. Select Book now for more details, or contact us with questions!

Rafting Tours

Day Rafting Tour

A licensed White Water Rafting Tour Guide will take you on a day trip down some of the prettiest waters you'll see in the Colorado Mountains. We will provide all of the gear and transportation to the designated starting location. A driver will pick you up at the end of the tour and take you back to the designated meeting place. Equipment will be included. Select Book now for more details, or contact us with questions!

Rafting and Camping Tour

You can select one of our different-length camping tours. For the 4-day camping trip, you will select one or two days to white water raft. For the 8-day Camping Tour, you will choose three or four days to white water raft. Equipment is provided. You can select book now for more information or contact us with any questions.
